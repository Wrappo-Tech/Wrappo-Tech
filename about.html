First step In Food Industry
Wrappo's initial move into the food industry started off with the popular Quick Service Restaurant (QSR) model.
Known for delivering fast, convenient, and high-quality meals, this model has been consumers favorite among decade.
As a key player in the QSR space, Wrappo combines deep industry knowledge and the strength of an established brands, securing a loyal customer base and a solid operational approach in the competitive food industry.
